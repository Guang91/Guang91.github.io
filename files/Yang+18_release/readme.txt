Data release for
  Yang et al. (2018), MNRAS, 475(2), 1887 
  Version 1.1, 04/19/2018
  Any questions should be sent to Guang Yang, gyang206265@gmail.com 

Data explanation:
  Yang+18_catalog.txt
    The source catalog used in Yang+18
  BHAR_SFR_vs_Mstar
    The folder containing data for <BHAR> & <BHAR>/<SFR> vs. logM_star (Figs.9 & 13 in Yang+18)
  BHAR_SFR_vs_Mstar_forSF
    The folder containing data for <BHAR> & <BHAR>/<SFR> vs. logM_star for star-forming galaxies (Figs.10 & 14 in Yang+18)
  Mbh_vs_Mstar
    The folder containing data for M_BH-M_star relation (Figs.15 & 16 in Yang+18)
