The M_BH-M_star relation for Figs.15 & 16 in
  Yang et al. (2018), MNRAS, 475(2), 1887 
  Version 1.0, 02/19/2018
  Any questions should be sent to Guang Yang, gyang206265@gmail.com 


Data explanation:
  File names: ini_ratio_*/z#.txt
    *, the assumed initial (z=4) M_star/M_BH ratio
    #, redshift
  In each z#.txt file:
    logM_star, total stellar mass (units: M_sun) in logarithm 
    logM_BHM, black hole mass (units: M_sun) in logarithm
